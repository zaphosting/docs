## Create account
Creating a ZAP-Hosting account is the first step to access and manage all services and features. The registration process is quick and only requires a few details.

To start the registration, visit the ZAP-Hosting website and navigate to the start page. There you will find the login area with a **Sign up!** button. Click on **Sign up!** to open the registration popup window.

![img](https://screensaver01.zap-hosting.com/index.php/s/5b6Wzc27CcHW3Zx/download)


Within the popup window, you must define the following information:

- Username
- Email address
- Password

After entering your details, confirm the security captcha. This step is required as a protection against automated registrations. Once all fields are completed and the captcha is confirmed, submit the registration form to create your account.

![img](https://screensaver01.zap-hosting.com/index.php/s/k6P3wSKWWRRiYax/preview)

## Account activation

After submitting the registration form, an activation email is sent to the provided email address. This email contains an activation code that must be confirmed to complete the account setup.

The delivery of the activation email may take a few minutes. If the message does not appear in your inbox, please also check your spam or junk mail folder.

:::warning Limited functionality without activation
You can log in immediately after registration. However, to access all features and services, the activation email must be confirmed.
:::
