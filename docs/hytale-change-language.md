## Introduction

If you are looking for your preferred language in Hytale, you may be hoping to improve your overall experience on a server by playing in a language you are more comfortable with. Especially during Early Access, understanding menus, items, quests, and system messages can make a significant difference when exploring the game or playing on multiplayer servers.

Since Hytale currently only supports English by default, additional languages are provided by the community through language packs. However, this can be achieved through the game client. 


## Installing language pack

Since Hytale currently only supports English by default, additional languages are provided by the community through language packs. These language packs are usually distributed as mods and can be downloaded from platforms such as CurseForge.

![img](https://screensaver01.zap-hosting.com/index.php/s/Wwim4MnYTEiiYw3/download)

To install a language pack, first download the desired language mod from CurseForge and make sure it is compatible with your current Hytale version. After downloading, place the mod file into the local Hytale mods directory on your computer.

![img](https://screensaver01.zap-hosting.com/index.php/s/EWpcEkgTegAZALp/download)

Once the language pack is placed in the correct folder, start or restart the Hytale client. Next, open the game settings, navigate to G and select your newly added language. 

![img](https://screensaver01.zap-hosting.com/index.php/s/CNQ2gpXAEFHESfc/download)
